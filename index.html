About

I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at North Carolina State University, co-advised by Prof. Aritra Mitra and Prof. Robert W. Heath. My research focuses on reinforcement learning, federated learning, and multi-agent systems, with an emphasis on sample efficiency and theoretical guarantees. I am interested in scalable and decentralized learning under limited data and communication constraints.

Research Interests
