About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at North Carolina State University, co-advised by Prof. Aritra Mitra and Prof. Robert W. Heath Jr.. My research focuses on reinforcement learning, federated learning, and multi-agent systems, with an emphasis on sample efficiency and theoretical guarantees. I am interested in scalable and decentralized learning under limited data and communication constraints.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Federated Learning
  • Multi-agent Systems
  • Decentralized Optimization
  • Sample-efficient Learning
  • High-probability Guarantees
